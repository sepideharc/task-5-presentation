Iran ایران

Iran also formerly known as Persia is located in Western Asia.

Prehistoric civilizations

Iran is home to one of the world's oldest civilizations, beginning with permenant settlements and villages from the tenth millenium BCE and then the beginning of cities and formation of first states during the fourth millennium BCE.

Achaemenid Empire

The Persian Empire reached its greatest extent during the Achaemenid period founded by Cyrus the Great in the sixth century BCE, stretching from Eastern Europe to the Indus Valley, becoming a larger empire than previously ever existed in the world.

Antiquty Empires

The Iranian realm fell to Alexander the Great in the fourth century BC, but reemerged shortly after as the Parthian Empire, followed by the Sasanian Empire, which became a leading world power for the next four centuries.

Islamic Era

Arab Muslims conquered Iran in the seventh century AD, ultimately leading to the displacement of the indigenous faith of Zoroastrianism with Islam. Unlike many other civilizations that changed their language to Arabic through accepting Islam, the Persian language never changed in Iran. Iran made major contributions to the Islamic Golden Age that followed, producing many influential figures in art and science.

Geography

Iran has a very diverese geography. You can ski in the Zagros mountains (in fact some of the best ski resorts in the world are in Iran) and then drive about three hours to the shores of the Persian gulf and swim under the sunshine.

Persian Cheetah

The wildlife of Iran is composed of several animal species the most famous one and my favorite is the Persian Cheetah.

Cuisine

Due to its variety of ethnic groups , the cuisine of Iran is very diverse. Herbs are frequently used, along with fruits such as plums, pomegranate, quince, prunes, apricots, and raisins. To achieve a balanced taste, spices such as saffron, dried lime, and cinnamon are mixed delicately to be used in various dishes.

Sports

Soccer is the most popular sport in Iran. The men's national team have won the Asian Cup on three occasions.They are going to be one of the 53 teams competeing in the upcoming 2018 FIFA World Cup in Russia.
